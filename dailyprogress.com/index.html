Ownership and Financing

The Daily Progress has been owned since 2012 by BH Media Group Inc., a subsidiary of publicly traded, Omaha, Nebraska-based Berkshire Hathaway, which is run by billionaire investor Warren Buffett. BH Media Group owns more than 30 daily newspapers, including its flagship publication, The Omaha World-Herald. It also owns more than 70 weekly newspapers and other printed publications in the United States, as well as one television station.

In July 2018, Lee Enterprises, a publicly traded newspaper chain that owns more than 40 daily newspapers, began managing operations for BH Media’s newspapers under a partnership announced by the two companies.

BH Media generates revenue from advertising and subscriptions for its printed publications and digital products. Its publications have won numerous awards for their journalism, including a Pulitzer Prize in 2018.

Dailyprogress.com runs advertising and charges readers after they have read a limited number of free articles.

Content

The Daily Progress covers politics, government, crime, business, education, and sports in Charlottesville, Virginia. A News subsection is devoted to news about the aftermath of the 2017 white nationalist rally in Charlottesville that claimed the life of a counter-protester. Other sections include Cavalier Insider, containing news about the University of Virginia, and Reader’s Choice Awards, naming winners of reader surveys on a wide range of cultural activities, professional services, products, and more.

The site also publishes articles from other BH Media publications and The Associated Press.

Credibility

Articles on the site are typically well-reported and balanced. Headlines are straightforward and accurately reflect the content. Dailyprogress.com publishes correction notices on articles, describing what was corrected. Opinion is distinguished from news content.

In 2018, the outlet was awarded the Pulitzer Prize for breaking news photography, for a photo showing the fatal car crash that killed Heather Heyer during the “Unite the Right” rally.

Transparency

A page on DailyProgress.com identifies and provides an email address and phone number for The Daily Progress’ publisher, editor, and the rest of the editorial staff.  Authors are identified on story pages, with contact information.

The About Us page discloses that BH Media Group bought the newspaper in 2012.

History

The Daily Progress was founded in 1892.
